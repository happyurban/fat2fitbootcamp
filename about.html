WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO?

Our country is currently facing an upward surge of non-communicable diseases such as diabetes and cardiovascular diseases. It is due as the number of people with obesity increases. This is supported by a study published in the British Medical Journal, The Lancet showed that Malaysia is the most obese country in Asia. In one study done by the Ministry of Health Malaysia, National Health & Morbidity Survey (NHMS) 2015 showed that nearly half of the Malaysian population aged 18 and above (47.7%) were overweight or obese. What's more, the national prevalence of obesity had increased by 2.6% respectively as compared to the previous findings of NHMS 2011.


This ALARMING trend of obesity epidemic in Malaysia does require an IMMEDIATE ACTION.


We at happyurban BELIEVE in enriching lives. The way we enriching lives is by providing full supportive programs for urban people to work towards practicing healthier lifestyle.
