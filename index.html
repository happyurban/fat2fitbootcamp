HOW TO PARTICIPATE?

1. Register as a Silver Contestant, Gold Contestant or Platinum Contestant before 16 SEP 2017.
2. Proud Malaysian aged 18 years and above regardless of gender and race.
3. Lived, studying and/or working in Kuala Lumpur or Selangor area. 
4. Body Mass Index (BMI) of at least more than 27.50 (Class Obese I) by the late of 23 SEP 2017.

BMI Calculation = weight (kg) / (height X height) (m²)

Example: 80.0kg / (1.50m X 1.50m) = 35.56

Malaysian Clinical Practice Guidelines of Obesity (2004) BMI Classes (kg/m²):
Underweight <18.50  |  Normal 18.50-22.99  |  Overweight 23.00-27.49
Obese I 27.50-34.99  |  Obese II 35.00-39.9  |  Obese III >40.00
