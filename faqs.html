FAQs

Frequently asked questions

1. What is FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP is a body transformation program targeted to a number of obese people in urban city of KL. 


2. How many obese people are targeted for FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

We are seeking for 35 obese people. 


3. Why HAPPYURBAN organizing FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

Why not? We are simply providing a platform for these urban people to work towards practicing healthier lifestyle.


4. How many phases are in the FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

There are two (2) phases. Firstly, CONTEST PHASE. Secondly, MAINTENANCE PHASE.


5. What is CONTEST PHASE?

CONTEST PHASE is an eight (8) weeks transformation contest of BOOTCAMP group classes and personal training sessions with a total winning cash prizes of RM14,000.00.


6. Where will the CONTEST PHASE be held at?

Bukit Kiara Sports Complex, Kuala Lumpur.


7. When will the CONTEST PHASE started?

From 23 SEP 2017 till 25 NOV 2017.


8. What are the prizes for the CONTEST?

Cash prizes of RM8,000.00 X1 for the first prize, RM4,000.00 X1 for the second prize, and RM2,000.00 X1 for the third prize.


9. Who are the transformational coaches in CONTEST PHASE?

A team of experienced Fitness Coaches, Mindfulness Coach, Dietitian, and Medical Officer.


10. How to participate in the CONTEST?

  • Register as a SILVER CONTESTANT, GOLD CONTESTANT or PLATINUM CONTESTANT before 16 SEP 2017.
  • Proud Malaysian aged 18 years and above regardless of gender and race.
  • Lived, studying and/or working in Kuala Lumpur or Selangor area. 
  • Body Mass Index (BMI) of at least more than 27.50 (Class Obese I) by the late of 23 SEP 2017.


11. When will be the closing date of registration for the CONTEST?

16 SEP 2017.


12. How much are the registration fees for the CONTEST?

Affordable prices of RM799 for SILVER CONTESTANT, RM1199 for GOLD CONTESTANT, and RM1599 for PLATINUM CONTESTANT.


13. What are the benefits of being a SILVER CONTESTANT?

A SILVER CONTESTANT will receive:

  • GROUP TRAINING of 3 days per week. Total of 24 classes in 8 weeks.
  • 2 Health Screenings (pre-intervention and post-intervention) inclusive of blood profile, BP measurement, anthropometric measurement, and fitness assessment. *
  • Doctor consultation.
  • Nutrition workshop.
  • Mindfulness workshop.

 

14. What are the benefits of being a GOLD CONTESTANT?

A GOLD CONTESTANT will receive:

  • PERSONAL TRAINING of 2 days per week. Total of 16 personal sessions in 8 weeks.
  • 2 Health Screenings (pre-intervention and post-intervention) inclusive of blood profile, BP measurement, anthropometric measurement, and fitness assessment. *
  • Doctor consultation.
  • Nutrition workshop.
  • Mindfulness workshop.


 15. What are the benefits of being a PLATINUM CONTESTANT?

A PLATINUM CONTESTANT will receive a combination of:

  • GROUP TRAINING 3 days per week and PERSONAL TRAINING 2 days per week. Total of 24 classes and 16 personal sessions in 8 weeks. 
  • 2 Health Screenings (pre-intervention and post-intervention) inclusive of blood profile, BP measurement, anthropometric measurement, and fitness assessment. *
  • Doctor consultation.
  • Nutrition workshop.
  • Mindfulness workshop.


16. Where and when is the timing for the FIRST DAY FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

The venue will be placed at Lecture Room 3 of Bukit Kiara Sports Complex on 23 SEP 2017 from 9.00am till 12.00pm.


17. Where and when are the timings for the GROUP TRAINING?

The venue will be placed at Multipurpose Court of Bukit Kiara Sports Complex. Contestants may either attend 6.00pm - 7.00pm BOOTCAMP Group Class or 8.00pm - 9.00pm BOOTCAMP Group Class from 25 SEP 2017 till 20 NOV 2017. 


18. Where and when are the timings for the PERSONAL TRAINING?

PERSONAL TRAINING is a one (1) hour personal session. On weekdays, the venue will be placed at Bukit Kiara Sports Complex Gymnasium from 9.00am -  9.00pm. While on weekends, it will be at Perdana Botanical Garden from 7.00am - 8.00pm. Sessions are from 25 SEP 2017 till 20 NOV 2017. 


19. Where and when is the timing for the WORKSHOPS?

The venue will be placed at  Lecture Room 3 of Bukit Kiara Sports Complex on 30 SEP 2017 from 9.00am till 1.00pm.


20. Where and when is the timing for the FINAL DAY FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?

The venue will be placed at Bukit Kiara Sports Complex Main Hall on 25 NOV 2017 from 9.00am till 12.30pm.


21. How are the winners of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP CONTEST will be selected?

The winners of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP CONTEST will be selected based on the percentage improvement of the health parameters in the Health Screening (pre-intervention and post-intervention) done. So, it is not just merely in losing weight. It is more in getting FIT. Thus, this is why this program is called as FAT2FIT.


22. Will all the contestant receive their performance data at the end of the CONTEST?

Yes. They will receive their Personal Performance Report Card via personal email before 11 DEC 2017.

A mixture of Bahasa Malaysia & English.

29. What are BOOTCAMP workouts actually designed for?

BOOTCAMP workouts are designed to improve an overall fitness inclusive of strength, cardio, flexibility, agility and endurance for any ages! Never in each of the sessions will be the same. Expect different challenges in every class.

AB - SO - LUTE... YES!  Of course, with the ideal combination of real workouts from the Fitness Coaches, the positive mindset lessons from the Mindfulness Coach, the right diet advices from the Dietitian, and the health consultation from the Medical Officer. We cover it all.
