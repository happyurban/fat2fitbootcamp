FAQS

1. What is FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?


FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP is a body transformation program targeted to a number of obese people in urban city of KL. 




2. How many obese people are targeted for FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?


We are seeking for 35 obese people. 




3. Why HAPPYURBAN organizing FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?


Why not? We are simply providing a platform for these urban people to work towards practicing healthier lifestyle.




4. How many phases are in the FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?


There are two (2) phases. Firstly, CONTEST PHASE. Secondly, MAINTENANCE PHASE.




5. What is CONTEST PHASE?


CONTEST PHASE is an eight (8) weeks transformation contest of BOOTCAMP group classes and personal training sessions with a total winning cash prizes of RM14,000.00.




6. Where will the CONTEST PHASE be held at?


Bukit Kiara Sports Complex, Kuala Lumpur.




7. When will the CONTEST PHASE started?


Year 2018.




8. What are the prizes for the CONTEST?


Cash prizes of RM8,000.00 X1 for the first prize, RM4,000.00 X1 for the second prize, and RM2,000.00 X1 for the third prize.




9. Who are the transformational coaches in CONTEST PHASE?


A team of experienced Fitness Coaches, Mindfulness Coach, Dietitian, and Medical Officer.




10. How to participate in the CONTEST?


Register as a SILVER CONTESTANT, GOLD CONTESTANT or PLATINUM CONTESTANT.

Proud Malaysian aged 18 years and above regardless of gender and race.

Lived, studying and/or working in Kuala Lumpur or Selangor area. 

Body Mass Index (BMI) of at least more than 27.50 (Class Obese I).




11. How are the winners of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP CONTEST will be selected?


The winners of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP CONTEST will be selected based on the percentage improvement of the health parameters in the Health Screening (pre-intervention and post-intervention) done. So, it is not just merely in losing weight. It is more in getting FIT. Thus, this is why this program is called as FAT2FIT.




12. Will all the contestant receive their performance data at the end of the CONTEST?


Yes. They will receive their Personal Performance Report Card.




13. Can someone with an advanced stage of disease join the CONTEST?


The answer is no. This program is targeted for the at-risk people who are currently obese. 




14. Can someone aged below 18 years old join the CONTEST?


Unfortunately, no. Somewhat, we are in planning of FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP: KIDS & TEEN EDITION in Late 2018 for our upcoming event. Stay updated!




15. What are BOOTCAMP workouts actually designed for?


BOOTCAMP workouts are designed to improve an overall fitness inclusive of strength, cardio, flexibility, agility and endurance for any ages! Never in each of the sessions will be the same. Expect different challenges in every class.




16. Do BOOTCAMP workouts really help obese people to lose weight safely and successfully and permanently keep it off for good?


AB - SO - LUTE... YES! Of course, with the ideal combination of real workouts from the Fitness Coaches, the positive mindset lessons from the Mindfulness Coach, the right diet advices from the Dietitian, and the health consultation from the Medical Officer. We cover it all. 




17. What are the attributes of these 35 obese people that happyurban are looking for in the FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP?


Someone who are - READY TO START, READY TO GO BEYOND, READY TO TRANSFORM, READY TO BE GREATER. Just like in our FAT2FIT BOOTCAMP Promo Video.




18. Owh! How about the MAINTENANCE PHASE? What is that?


MAINTENANCE PHASE is a phase designed for the continuation of CONTEST PHASE. It is a sixteen (16) weeks supportive program with a once per week of PERSONAL TRAINING. Total of 16 personal sessions in 16 weeks.
